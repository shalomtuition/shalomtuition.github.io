Guidelines of tuition group

The intention of the guidelines ensure the efficiency and effectiveness of this tuition group.

  1. To develop good character and academic success in all the students

2. Lesson

  1. If the student misses a lesson, he/she needs to replace it within 7 days for free.
  2. Missed or cancelled lessons are chargable.

3. Payment

  1. This tuition group only collect fee using cashless methods.

  3. Certain month will have five weeks. Hence, the fee will be prorated.
  4. All fees is listed at http://shalomtuition.com/fee

4. Referral

  1. You can refer students to this group and get a referral fee of $50.
